# Midnight Galaxy

A dramatic and cosmic theme with deep purples and mystical tones for impactful presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Purple**: `#2b1e3e` - Rich dark base
- **Cosmic Blue**: `#4a4e8f` - Mystical mid-tone
- **Lavender**: `#a490c2` - Soft accent color
- **Silver**: `#e6e6fa` - Light highlights and text

## Typography

- **Headers**: FreeSans Bold
- **Body Text**: FreeSans

## Best Used For

Entertainment industry, gaming presentations, nightlife venues, luxury brands, creative agencies.

## Role Mapping

- **Background**: Deep Purple for covers, hero fields, and immersive sections.
- **Primary Text**: Silver on dark fields; near-black text on light exported surfaces only.
- **Accent**: Cosmic Blue for navigation, chart anchors, and structured highlights.
- **Highlight**: Lavender for glow accents, not body text on dark backgrounds unless contrast is verified.
- **Divider/Frame**: Lavender or Silver at low opacity for cinematic structure.

## Application Notes

Use this theme for dramatic, premium, night-mode artifacts. Avoid excessive glow effects; the premium feel comes from contrast discipline and precise spacing.
