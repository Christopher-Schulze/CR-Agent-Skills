# Arctic Frost

A cool and crisp winter-inspired theme that conveys clarity, precision, and professionalism.

## Color Palette

- **Ice Blue**: `#d4e4f7` - Light backgrounds and highlights
- **Steel Blue**: `#4a6fa5` - Primary accent color
- **Silver**: `#c0c0c0` - Metallic accent elements
- **Crisp White**: `#fafafa` - Clean backgrounds and text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Healthcare presentations, technology solutions, winter sports, clean tech, pharmaceutical content.

## Role Mapping

- **Background**: Crisp White for clean page fields; Ice Blue for soft panels or sectional bands.
- **Primary Text**: Darkened Steel Blue or near-black neutral for body text when contrast is required.
- **Accent**: Steel Blue for key actions, headers, chart anchors, and section markers.
- **Divider/Frame**: Silver for rules, table borders, and low-emphasis structure.
- **Highlight**: Ice Blue for callouts; keep highlights quiet and clinical.

## Application Notes

Use this theme for precise, calm, evidence-driven artifacts. Avoid making the whole artifact pale blue; preserve enough white and dark text contrast for a crisp premium feel.
