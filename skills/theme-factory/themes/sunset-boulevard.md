# Sunset Boulevard

A warm and vibrant theme inspired by golden hour sunsets, perfect for energetic and creative presentations.

## Color Palette

- **Burnt Orange**: `#e76f51` - Primary accent color
- **Coral**: `#f4a261` - Secondary warm accent
- **Warm Sand**: `#e9c46a` - Highlighting and backgrounds
- **Deep Purple**: `#264653` - Dark contrast and text

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Creative pitches, marketing presentations, lifestyle brands, event promotions, inspirational content.

## Role Mapping

- **Background**: Warm Sand for lively fields; Deep Purple for high-impact title moments.
- **Primary Text**: Deep Purple for readable contrast.
- **Accent**: Burnt Orange for focal actions and campaign energy.
- **Secondary Accent**: Coral for softer emphasis and supporting highlights.
- **Divider/Frame**: Warm Sand-darkened rules or low-opacity Deep Purple.

## Application Notes

Use this theme for energetic, expressive artifacts. Avoid stacking all warm colors at once; give the palette a dark anchor and clear hierarchy.
